Recipe #1: Ravioli

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Dough 500 grams
Ricotta 1 packet
Spinach 200 grams
Tomatoes 1 cup sliced

Step 1: Slice the tomatoes
Step 2: Melt the ricotta
Step 3: Slice the spinach
Step 4: Fold the dough in the desired shape
Step 5: Put the tomatoes, spinach and ricotta in the dough
Step 6: Place the raviolis in the freezer for 1 day
Step 7: Take them out, boil them and enjoy.

Recipe #2: Pizza

  • Time: 3 hours
  • Serves: 5
Ingredients Quantity
Water 1.5 cups
Yeast 1 packet
Flour 490 grams
Tomatoes 2 pieces
Extravirgin Olive Oil 2 tablespoons

Step 1: Proof yeast.
Step 2: Mix flour, proofed yeast and virgin olive oil.
Step 3: Let the dough rise for one hour.
Step 4: Blend the tomatoes to make a tomato sauce.
Step 5: Stretch out the dough ball.
Step 6: Apply the tomato sauce.
Step 7: Bake for an hour and enjoy.

Recipe #3

  • Time: 3 hours
  • Serves: 10
Ingredients Quantity
Double Cream 284 ml
Full Fat Milk 300 ml
Sugar 115 grams
Tomatoes 2 pieces
Vanilla Pod 1 piece
Free Range Egg Yolks 3
Ice Cubes 20+

Step 1: Pour Milk, Cream and half the vanilla pod in the pan.
Step 2: Heat mixture and set aside for 30 minutes.
Step 3: Beat egg yolks.
Step 4: Add yolks to mixture.
Step 5: Mix all ingredients.
Step 6: Put them in the freezer.
Step 7: Enjoy!
